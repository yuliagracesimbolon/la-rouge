CHANEL
ROUGE ALLURE VELVET COMÉTE

Description

Comets reflect light with great intensity. They follow their own trajectory and never fade away in the eye of the beholder. A symbol of good luck, the star with 5 irregular points inspired Coco Chanel's first and only High Jewellery collection. Today, a comet comes into view on the iconic ROUGE ALLURE VELVET case and leaves its radiant mark on each lip colour. 8 limited-edition exclusive creations with a new, delicately pearly matte finish and luminous sculpting effect.
Composition :
• Ultra-fine, pure, and highly concentrated pigments for intense color
• A combination of soft-focus microspheres and ultra-fine pearly particles for a finish that is both matte and luminous
• A jojoba oil derivative for smooth lips. How to Use :
Apply directly to the lips.
For perfectly defined lips, wear ROUGE ALLURE VELVET
with LE CRAYON LÈVRES liner in the same shade.
Then, apply the lipstick using the brush from the lip pencil.
